About Us

RAAJ CONSULTANCY SERVICES is a dynamic and results-driven consultancy firm based in Bangalore, India. Established with a vision to deliver excellence, we specialize in providing firewall security services, manpower consultancy, project support services, and custom software solutions tailored for the telecom and IT infrastructure sectors.

We are committed to delivering high-quality services through a client-focused approach, combining industry best practices with innovative technologies.
